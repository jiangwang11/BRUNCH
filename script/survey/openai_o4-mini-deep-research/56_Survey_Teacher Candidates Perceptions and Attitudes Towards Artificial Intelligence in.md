# 引言  
近年来生成式人工智能（尤其是ChatGPT）的出现引发了教育界对人工智能（AI）教学应用的广泛关注。研究指出，AI技能“可能成为21世纪最重要的科技能力”之一，ChatGPT于2022年11月发布后引发了公众对其教育影响的极大兴趣 ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=GenAI%20is%20proving%20to%20be,1))。得益于AI，教育者可以提供个性化学习体验、自适应教材生成和学生的实时支持 ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=GenAI%20is%20proving%20to%20be,1))。然而，要将AI有效融入课堂，需要教师具有相关技术素养和责任伦理意识。针对教师候选人（即师范生）对AI的态度与感知的研究对于指导师范教育改革至关重要。本综述聚焦2022–2025年间师范生群体对教育中AI的认知、态度及其传播研究成果，对不同研究方法类别下的代表性工作进行梳理，并总结其中的共性结论与未来趋势。

# 研究方法类别与代表性工作  
**定量接受度模型研究。** 这一类研究通常基于技术接受模型（TAM）或统一接受与使用技术模型（UTAUT）等理论构建问卷。Zhang等人（2023）采用TAM3构建模型，以452名德国师范生为样本，运用结构方程模型（SEM）分析预备教教师对基于AI教育应用的使用意图 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Based%20on%20a%20prominent%20technology,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of))。结果表明，“感知易用性”（β=0.297***)和“感知有用性”（β=0.501***)是影响师范生AI使用意图的主要因素 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Based%20on%20a%20prominent%20technology,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of)) ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=match%20at%20L816%20findings%20suggest,technology%20acceptance%20have%20also%20shown))。此外，研究发现性别差异显著：女性师范生对AI使用表现出更高的愉悦感，而男性则对AI表现出更低的焦虑 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Furthermore%2C%20the%20latent%20mean%20differences,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of))。Adigun等人（2025）在UTAUT框架下考察了411名尼日利亚包容性教育专业师范生对AI的采用意图，其SEM分析发现“努力期望”（即认为使用AI容易）对采纳意图具有直接正向影响，而技术自我效能感对意图没有直接效应，只通过“绩效期望”和“社会影响”间接影响AI的采纳 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/education/articles/10.3389/feduc.2025.1519472/full#:~:text=Results%3A%20The%20study%20established%20that,and%20use%20AI%20for%20inclusive))。这类研究结论一致认为，提高师范生的AI知识、体验与自我效能，将促进其未来教师角色中AI工具的应用意愿。

**混合方法研究。** 一些研究结合问卷调查与访谈，以多层面探讨师范生的AI认知。Malacapay（2025）对菲律宾某高校46名小学教育师范生进行了问卷与深度访谈研究。研究结果显示，被访者总体AI素养“略高于平均水平”，对将AI融入课堂持开放态度，但对实际使用仍有不确定感（例如对数据安全、道德规范等问题存在顾虑） ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。回归分析表明，师范生的“准备度”（如现有的AI技能和信心）比单纯的AI知识更能预测他们使用AI的内在动机 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。访谈揭示的主题包括：师范生对AI基础知识存在概念性差距、既怀有热情又有不确定性、关注伦理问题、缺乏系统指导、强烈期望将AI整合进教学等 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。这些发现提示师范院校应在课程中加入结构化且注重伦理的AI培训，以培养师范生成为“自信、负责任且创新”的未来教育者 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。

**质性研究。** 部分学者通过案例研究或叙事调研捕捉师范生对AI的深层次看法。Guan等（2025）以24名香港地区师范生为访谈对象，进行主题分析。他们发现师范生通常“只在确有需要时才使用AI”，且“在AI基础原理和伦理方面理解不足”，往往将AI“机械地视为工具而非教育中的动态合作者” ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/386515736_Pre-service_Teachers_Preparedness_for_AI-integrated_Education_An_Investigation_from_Perceptions_Capabilities_and_Teachers%27_Identity_Changes#:~:text=AI,interviews%20with%20a%20sample%20of))。这些访谈表明，师范生对AI技术持务实态度但缺乏批判意识，需要提高其对教师角色将随AI改变的认识。Karataş和Yüce（2024）采用叙事探究法对141名土耳其师范生进行ChatGPT体验反思，结果揭示ChatGPT在**提升教学效率和个性化学习**方面具有潜力，但同时也引发了严重的**学术诚信**和信息准确性担忧 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical))。研究强调必须在师范生培训中融入全面的AI伦理教育框架，以平衡AI工具的优势与潜在风险 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical))。

**量表与工具开发。** 有研究专注于设计用于评估教师候选人AI相关态度的测量工具。Gómez-García等（2025）在西班牙构建了一份25项调查量表，包括“负责任的AI使用态度、教材创作的创造力、使用愉悦度、学习焦虑程度”等五个潜在因子 ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=their%20teaching%20profession%2C%20taking%20into,the%20learning%20of%20this%20emerging))。经统计分析验证，该量表具有良好的信度和效度，可作为衡量师范生AI使用感知的心理测量工具 ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=instrument,classified%20in%20five%20latent%20factors)) ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=their%20teaching%20profession%2C%20taking%20into,the%20learning%20of%20this%20emerging))。Işık等（2024）在土耳其也开发了一个37题的教师AI工具感知量表，经过因子分析提取出三大因子并验证其高可靠性 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/387593848_Teachers%27_Perception_Scale_Towards_the_Use_of_Artificial_Intelligence_Tools_in_Education#:~:text=the%20scale%20with%20the%20collected,660%20and))，为后续大规模调研提供了可用的评价工具。这类工作为后续关于师范生AI态度的研究提供了标准化测量手段。

# 实验与评价总结  
上述各类研究揭示了师范生对教育中AI的普遍态度特点：总体上师范生对AI持**积极乐观**态度，期盼其在个性化学习、效率提升等方面的潜力 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical)) ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/378079404_Pre-service_teachers%27_attitudes_towards_artificial_intelligence_and_its_integration_into_EFL_teaching_and_learning#:~:text=encouraging%20to%20learn%20that%2064.24,agreed%20that%20EFL%20teachers%20might))；但同时，他们对自身AI知识和应用能力普遍信心不足，对AI的伦理、学术诚信风险等表现出明显关注 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical)) ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。影响师范生AI接受意图的关键因素包括“感知有用性/易用性”“努力期望”等技术感知因素，以及“AI素养水平”“自我效能”等技能认知因素（上述研究模型分析结果一致指向这些核心变量 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Based%20on%20a%20prominent%20technology,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of)) ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/education/articles/10.3389/feduc.2025.1519472/full#:~:text=Results%3A%20The%20study%20established%20that,and%20use%20AI%20for%20inclusive))）。此外，多数研究发现师范生把AI看作**辅助工具**而非教学主体，它能够辅助教学设计和资源开发，但需要教师批判性运用 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/386515736_Pre-service_Teachers_Preparedness_for_AI-integrated_Education_An_Investigation_from_Perceptions_Capabilities_and_Teachers%27_Identity_Changes#:~:text=AI,interviews%20with%20a%20sample%20of)) ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical))。综上，尽管具体研究方法和背景不同，各项工作普遍得出：若要提升师范生AI应用意愿，需在师范教育中增强AI基础教育、实践经验和伦理素养训练，并关注个体差异（如性别、专业等）对AI接纳的影响 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Based%20on%20a%20prominent%20technology,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of)) ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))。

# 趋势与挑战  
1. **AI素养课程与伦理教育的融合。** 随着生成式AI工具应用的普及，未来师范教育将越来越重视系统化的AI素养培训和道德教育框架的构建（参见Karataş & Yüce 2024的建议 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical))）。预计研究将向开发针对师范生的AI课程模型、教学方案和评估指标方向发展。  
2. **基于生成式AI的教学实验研究增多。** 近期已有研究探索ChatGPT等工具在师范生教学中的具体应用与反馈（如Karataş & Yüce 2024 ([www.irrodl.org](https://www.irrodl.org/index.php/irrodl/article/download/7785/6079?inline=1#:~:text=reflective%20narratives%2C%20the%20study%20unveils,However%2C%20critical%20ethical))）。未来将有更多实证案例研究，评估AI辅助教学设计、课堂教学实习等项目对师范生教学技能和观点的影响。  
3. **差异化因素研究深入。** 已有证据表明性别、学科背景等影响AI态度 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00420-7#:~:text=Furthermore%2C%20the%20latent%20mean%20differences,and%20from%20perceived%20ease%20of))；未来趋势将进一步探讨文化背景、教学领域对师范生AI认知的影响，例如跨国比较研究和包容性教育背景下的AI采纳差异（Adigun 2025对包容教育的探讨即为例证 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/education/articles/10.3389/feduc.2025.1519472/full#:~:text=Results%3A%20The%20study%20established%20that,and%20use%20AI%20for%20inclusive))）。  
4. **多样化量表和大数据分析手段。** 随着AI研究工具的成熟，将出现更多用于量化评估师范生AI态度和准备度的量表（如Gómez-García等2025年 ([www.redalyc.org](https://www.redalyc.org/journal/3314/331481521012/#:~:text=their%20teaching%20profession%2C%20taking%20into,the%20learning%20of%20this%20emerging))所作尝试），并结合机器学习等数据挖掘方法进行大规模样本分析，以揭示更精细的影响机制。  
5. **教育政策与资源公平性关注。** 鉴于部分研究指出技术落差和指导不足问题（Malacapay 2025 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/395654352_AI_literacy_preparedness_and_use_motives_of_elementary_education_pre-_service_teachers_of_a_state_university_in_the_Philippines#:~:text=indicated%20that%20the%20participants%27%20AI,strong%20desire%20for%20AI%20integration))），未来课题还将关注如何在师范教育层面制定合理政策，确保所有师范生均有机会获得AI工具和培训资源。

# 结论  
近年研究表明，师范生普遍认识到AI在教育中的潜力，但对其技术原理和道德影响的理解尚浅。现有文献呼吁师范院校更新课程、强化实践训练，使未来教师能够批判而负责任地使用AI。本文综述了2022–2025年间代表性研究成果，在经验发现和未来趋势的指引下，为师范教育改革提供借鉴：必须突出培养师范生的AI素养和人机协同教学能力，同时关注伦理与公平性挑战，助力教育创新。
