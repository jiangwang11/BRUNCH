# 引言  
近年来，学术研究评估正快速引入人工智能（AI）技术，以缓解同行评审负担并提高评价效率 ([www.preprints.org](https://www.preprints.org/manuscript/202511.1542#:~:text=Recognizing%20this%20reviewer%20scarcity%2C%20many,style)) ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/research-metrics-and-analytics/articles/10.3389/frma.2025.1684137/full#:~:text=Results%3A%20The%20two%20LLMs%20demonstrated,65%2C%20p))。例如，面对每日数以千计的新论文涌现（2024年万维引文系统收录论文超250万篇），传统评审体系已捉襟见肘 ([www.preprints.org](https://www.preprints.org/manuscript/202511.1542#:~:text=Scientific%20publications%20have%20surged%20to,and%20concerns%20about%20review%20quality))。大型语言模型（LLM）和机器学习方法因此被应用于论文筛选、质量评估、推荐和成果预测等环节。一系列研究从不同角度探索了AI辅助下的研究评价问题：有的关注利用LLM生成或辅助同行评审意见 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=aUmtVlaPRD#:~:text=sensitive%20nature%20of%20the%20data,code%20and%20data%20is%20at)) ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/can-large-language-models-provide-useful#:~:text=of%20GPT,the%20field%20of%20AI%20and))，有的探索AI在系统综述和证据汇总中的应用，有的考察AI在科研资金评审与学术指标构建中的作用。本文概述2022–2025年代表性工作，按照方法类别进行归纳总结，并讨论实验评估共性结论与未来趋势。

## 方法分类与代表作  

### 同行评审自动化  
自动化同行评审旨在通过AI辅助或模拟传统评审流程，提升效率并缓解专家短缺。例如，Jin等提出**AgentReview**框架，利用LLM模拟审稿过程，从而分离影响评审决策的多种潜在因素 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=aUmtVlaPRD#:~:text=sensitive%20nature%20of%20the%20data,code%20and%20data%20is%20at))。该工作发现审稿人偏见导致论文决策存在显著差异（约37.1%变化），验证了社会影响论和权威偏差等理论 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=aUmtVlaPRD#:~:text=sensitive%20nature%20of%20the%20data,code%20and%20data%20is%20at))。Liang等构建了一条GPT-4驱动的评审反馈管线，对30多万篇来自顶级会议（Nature期刊系列、ICLR等）的论文生成评论，并与真实评审意见对比。研究显示，GPT-4生成的反馈与单个人类评审意见重叠率达30%–39%，与人审之间的互评均值相当；对低质论文的一致性更高 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/can-large-language-models-provide-useful#:~:text=of%20GPT,the%20field%20of%20AI%20and))。该工作还通过308名研究者的问卷调查发现，57.4%的用户认为GPT-4反馈“有帮助”，并且82.4%认为其至少比某些人类意见更有益 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/can-large-language-models-provide-useful#:~:text=reviewers%20is%20larger%20for%20the,help%20researchers%2C%20we%20also%20identify))。Huang等研究LLM在论文原创性评价中的能力，通过精心设计的prompt(提示)让多个LLM对生物医学论文进行打分。结果表明，LLM能够区分不同原创性水平的论文；GPT-4在可读性和逻辑性上表现最好 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0306457324003327#:~:text=score%20,of%20completeness%2C%20logicality%2C%20and%20regularity))。但研究也发现，LLM通常评判偏“宽松”（generous），这意味着在质量评分时存在倾向性 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0306457324003327#:~:text=score%20,of%20completeness%2C%20logicality%2C%20and%20regularity))。Zhuang等综述了自动化学术审稿（Automated Scholarly Paper Review, ASPR）的概念与技术发展，提出了ASPR管道模型并汇总了当前阶段LLM可解决的子任务 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S156625352300146X#:~:text=Peer%20review%20is%20a%20widely,the%20review%20and%20discussion%2C%20we)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1566253525004051#:~:text=need%20to%20be%20addressed,the%20researchers%20and%20can%20promote))。他们指出，虽然每个审稿阶段已有研究尝试AI工具（例如格式审查、贡献总结等），但ASPR面临数据不足、文档解析、深度推理等挑战 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S156625352300146X#:~:text=review%20the%20relevant%20literature%20and,to%20fully%20undertake%20the%20reviewing)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S156625352300146X#:~:text=ASPR%20with%20the%20existing%20technologies,the%20reviewing%20workload%20from%20humans))。

### 文献评估与系统综述  
AI同样被用于文献汇总和质量评价领域，以加速系统综述、元分析等任务。Tang等设计了评估框架，对照人类写作质量，评测LLM在**参考文献生成、摘要撰写和综述构建**等子任务上的表现 ([aclanthology.org](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.83/#:~:text=study%20introduces%20a%20framework%20to,when%20it%20comes%20to%20writing))。实验发现，尽管最新模型已取得进步，生成的参考文献仍存在“幻觉”问题（引用缺失或错误），而不同领域的模型性能差异显著 ([aclanthology.org](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.83/#:~:text=study%20introduces%20a%20framework%20to,when%20it%20comes%20to%20writing))。他们指出，需要进一步研究和优化，以提高LLM在自动撰写文献综述时的可靠性 ([aclanthology.org](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.83/#:~:text=writing%2C%20and%20literature%20review%20composition,LLMs%20in%20automating%20academic%20literature))。同样地，Wu等提出基于LLM的自动化审稿生成方法，在氢化丙烯烷基化催化剂领域进行了实证评估 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12125968/#:~:text=cognitive%20load,and%20citation%20integrity%2C%20while%20demonstrating))。该方法第二层面利用1041篇论文进行催化剂属性分析，展示了多层次质量控制流程可将LLM幻觉率降低至<0.5%。实验表明，自动生成的综述在质量上与人工撰写不相上下 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12125968/#:~:text=cognitive%20load,and%20citation%20integrity%2C%20while%20demonstrating))。此外，de la Torre-López等从工具与技术层面调研了过去15年中AI在系统文献综述中的应用 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00607-023-01181-x#:~:text=therefore%2C%20subject%20to%20automation%20by,an%20increasingly%20automated%20SLR%20process))。他们总结当前工具支持检索、筛选和结果提炼等功能，人工只能在流程规划和决策环节发挥作用。Bolaños等则对AI强化的系统综述工具进行了全面分析，指出LLM和知识图谱等高级技术正在进入该领域，但评估标准和可用性亟待改进 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-024-10902-3#:~:text=This%20paper%20presents%20a%20comprehensive,tools%20using%20a%20framework%20that)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-024-10902-3#:~:text=field%2C%20outlines%20key%20research%20challenges%2C,AI%20solutions%20in%20this%20field))。

### 资助评审与研究经费  
近年来，研究资助机构也开始尝试使用AI优化项目评审流程。Carbonell Cortés等报道了西班牙“La Caixa”基金会在其健康科研项目评审中，试点使用AI模型进行**冗余提案预筛选** ([www.cambridge.org](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/data-and-policy/article/aiassisted-prescreening-of-biomedical-research-proposals-ethical-considerations-and-the-pilot-case-of-la-caixa-foundation/D17CC86C884D4F9E3A6DD9ABF3F4605E#:~:text=The%20%E2%80%9Cla%20Caixa%E2%80%9D%20Foundation%20has,challenges%20and%20areas%20of%20improvement))。该系统自动识别“尚未成熟”的提案并建议人类审核者考虑剔除，从而减轻专家负担。研究分析了国内外的类似实践，并讨论了此举在伦理和法律框架下的可接受性 ([www.cambridge.org](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/data-and-policy/article/aiassisted-prescreening-of-biomedical-research-proposals-ethical-considerations-and-the-pilot-case-of-la-caixa-foundation/D17CC86C884D4F9E3A6DD9ABF3F4605E#:~:text=The%20%E2%80%9Cla%20Caixa%E2%80%9D%20Foundation%20has,challenges%20and%20areas%20of%20improvement))。Mangalam在观点文章中指出，现行资助评审机制系统性偏向资深和保守议题，阻碍了新生代创新 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1667752#:~:text=As%20artificial%20intelligence%20,reinforcing%20system%E2%80%94more))。他提出，AI可以打破这一“融资守门人”模式，通过算法评估释放更公正的机遇 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1667752#:~:text=patterns%20in%20biomedical%20sciences%2C%20we,more%20diverse%20wave%20of%20discovery))。未来可期的是，多阶段混合评审体系可能成为主流：初轮可由AI算法筛选或打分，后续流程结合专家评审 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1667752#:~:text=not%20only%20who%20gets%20to,more%20diverse%20wave%20of%20discovery))。总之，已有案例显示AI能在早期筛选和评分阶段辅助基金会决策，但人类监督和红线机制仍不可或缺 ([www.cambridge.org](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/data-and-policy/article/aiassisted-prescreening-of-biomedical-research-proposals-ethical-considerations-and-the-pilot-case-of-la-caixa-foundation/D17CC86C884D4F9E3A6DD9ABF3F4605E#:~:text=The%20%E2%80%9Cla%20Caixa%E2%80%9D%20Foundation%20has,challenges%20and%20areas%20of%20improvement)) ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1667752#:~:text=patterns%20in%20biomedical%20sciences%2C%20we,more%20diverse%20wave%20of%20discovery))。

## 实验与评价总结  
上述研究的实验结果呈现出一致的趋势。首先，AI技术大幅提升了评审效率：例如，Zhou等实验证明，LLM完成质量评价平均耗时仅33秒，而人类需1582秒，效率提升近48倍 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/research-metrics-and-analytics/articles/10.3389/frma.2025.1684137/full#:~:text=Results%3A%20The%20two%20LLMs%20demonstrated,65%2C%20p))。Liang等也报告GPT-4对论文的批注显著加快反馈速度且被多数作者认作有用 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/can-large-language-models-provide-useful#:~:text=reviewers%20is%20larger%20for%20the,help%20researchers%2C%20we%20also%20identify))。其次，AI评分与人类评审具有可观的一致性：常用指标下，LLM评分与专家评分的相关度较高，LLM提出的评审要点与人审重叠程度（30%–40%）接近人审间的一致性 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/can-large-language-models-provide-useful#:~:text=of%20GPT,4%20and%20human))。然而，跨研究也发现共同问题：LLM往往较宽容“高分”论文 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0306457324003327#:~:text=score%20,of%20completeness%2C%20logicality%2C%20and%20regularity))；模型评分对作者身份或心理暗示敏感（存在权威倾向） ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=aUmtVlaPRD#:~:text=sensitive%20nature%20of%20the%20data,code%20and%20data%20is%20at))；LLM生成的文本可能出现幻觉引用或对复杂方法识别不足 ([aclanthology.org](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.83/#:~:text=study%20introduces%20a%20framework%20to,when%20it%20comes%20to%20writing)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12125968/#:~:text=cognitive%20load,and%20citation%20integrity%2C%20while%20demonstrating))。因此，多数研究建议保持“人审+AI”并行模式：AI先行过滤和初审后，人类专家对关键步骤进行复核，以平衡效率与准确性 ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/research-metrics-and-analytics/articles/10.3389/frma.2025.1684137/full#:~:text=Conclusion%3A%20LLMs%20markedly%20reduce%20appraisal,quality%20appraisal%20in%20evidence%20synthesis)) ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=aUmtVlaPRD#:~:text=sensitive%20nature%20of%20the%20data,code%20and%20data%20is%20at))。

## 趋势与挑战  
展望2025年前后，AI辅助研究评价将沿多条趋势演进并面临挑战。其一，**人机混合评审机制**将更为普遍。如已有会议试点在首轮评审中添入AI评价结果 ([www.preprints.org](https://www.preprints.org/manuscript/202511.1542#:~:text=Recognizing%20this%20reviewer%20scarcity%2C%20many,style)); 未来可能形成AI初审+人工复核的混合流程，将评审成本分散于机器和专家之间。其二，**科研评价工具的治理与规范**将成为关键课题。相关研究警示，LLM得分若取代传统引用指标，可能诱使作者过度美化摘要 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11192-025-05361-8#:~:text=Language%20Models%20,based%20indicators%20may%20push%20them))；而LLM本身的偏见尚未揭露清楚 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11192-025-05361-8#:~:text=Language%20Models%20,based%20indicators%20may%20push%20them))。因此学界正呼吁建立可解释、可审计的评价框架和伦理准则，并开发AI检测与问责机制（如确保模型输出可追溯） ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11192-025-05361-8#:~:text=Language%20Models%20,based%20indicators%20may%20push%20them)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-024-10902-3#:~:text=This%20paper%20presents%20a%20comprehensive,tools%20using%20a%20framework%20that))。其三，**跨领域综合分析**将得以加强：LLM和知识图谱等技术的结合，使得对学术成果的全局评估成为可能（如Wu等提出的一键综述工具 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12125968/#:~:text=cognitive%20load,and%20citation%20integrity%2C%20while%20demonstrating))），未来预计会出现更多集成分析系统。此外，AI在科研资助和政策制定中的应用将更加深入 ([www.cambridge.org](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/data-and-policy/article/aiassisted-prescreening-of-biomedical-research-proposals-ethical-considerations-and-the-pilot-case-of-la-caixa-foundation/D17CC86C884D4F9E3A6DD9ABF3F4605E#:~:text=The%20%E2%80%9Cla%20Caixa%E2%80%9D%20Foundation%20has,challenges%20and%20areas%20of%20improvement)) ([www.frontiersin.org](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frai.2025.1667752#:~:text=patterns%20in%20biomedical%20sciences%2C%20we,more%20diverse%20wave%20of%20discovery))。整体而言，AI辅助研究评价的未来需权衡效率与公正、创新与规范，**信任度、可解释性和公平性**仍是核心挑战。

## 结论  
本文综述了近年来有关AI辅助科研评价的代表性研究工作。研究表明，AI技术已在论文评审、文献综述、项目筛选等领域展现出高效性能，并能在一定程度上接近专业评价结果。相应地，也暴露出幻觉风险、系统偏见和摸索不足等问题。综合各项工作，AI最可能以“人机协作”方式融入评价流程：AI完成海量文档初筛和定量分析，人类专家负责最终判断和复杂推理。在此过程中，建立严格的评估标准和监管机制，确保评价结果公正可信，是未来研究的重点方向。
