# 引言  
近年生成式人工智能（GenAI）在教育领域蓬勃兴起，自我调节学习（SRL）研究关注AI如何支持学习者自主调控。系统评述指出，AI工具能够促进SRL的预备、执行与反思阶段，但也提醒需谨慎平衡技术优势与学习者自我效能 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41539-025-00319-0#:~:text=feedback%20systems%2C%20serious%20games%2C%20and,research%20to%20optimize%20this%20synergy)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s41239-025-00548-8#:~:text=The%20main%20results%20revealed%20that,did%20not%20specify%20an%20SRL))。映射研究发现，现阶段AI–SRL研究主要面向高等教育，聚焦认知与元认知策略，而学习动机方面尚缺乏深入探索 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s41239-025-00548-8#:~:text=The%20main%20results%20revealed%20that,did%20not%20specify%20an%20SRL)) ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00406-5#:~:text=conducted%20with%2016%20university%20students,aim%20of%20supporting%20students%E2%80%99%20SRL))。本文综述2022–2025年的代表性研究，依照方法类别组织：聊天机器人/对话式代理、自适应辅导与反馈系统、基于游戏/沉浸式环境的学习工具、以及SRL过程分析与测评方法。

## 方法分类与代表作  
**1. 聊天机器人与对话式AI辅助**。Chiu等（2023）提出了基于目标设定、反馈与个性化的教育设计原则，将ChatGPT等会话AI纳入SRL循环中，并强调教师需了解其功能以恰当介入 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2071-1050/15/17/12921#:~:text=In%20our%20position%2C%20we%20argue,upon%20in%20the%20subsequent%20section))。同组学者进一步通过Delphi调研和自我决定理论，开发了一个包含20种ChatGPT学习活动的分类工具，将AI辅助活动与动力需求和SRL阶段对应起来 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11423-024-10366-w#:~:text=classification%20tool%20for%20learning%20activities,to%20develop%20guidelines%20for%20AI))，为研究者和教师设计AI驱动的SRL活动提供参考。Ng等（2024）在香港一项中学生科学学习实验中对比了基于ChatGPT的生成式AI聊天机器人（“SRLbot”）与传统规则型机器人（Nemobot），结果表明SRLbot显著提升了学生的科学知识、行为投入和学习动机 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/379199414_Empowering_student_self-regulated_learning_and_science_education_through_ChatGPT_A_pioneering_pilot_study#:~:text=learning%20experience%20with%2074%20Secondary,reduced%20learning%20anxiety%20and%20promoted))；生成式AI通过个性化推荐和即时反馈降低了学习焦虑，促进了长期学习习惯。Lai（2024）从过程挖掘视角提出将SRL模型应用于GenAI聊天环境：通过对学生与教育聊天机器人交互生成的提示（prompts）进行分类，并借助过程挖掘技术可视化和分析学习路径，为教育者提供有关学习者行为模式和改进建议 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/1999-5903/16/6/218#:~:text=This%20article%20seeks%20to%20adapt,applications%20of%20chatbots%20for%20SRL)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/1999-5903/16/6/218#:~:text=match%20at%20L402%20In%20this,and%20deviations%20from%20expected%20learning))。该研究还论证了使用Zimmerman的SRL多层模型评估ChatGPT在各个SRL阶段的潜力 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2071-1050/15/17/12921#:~:text=In%20our%20position%2C%20we%20argue,upon%20in%20the%20subsequent%20section))。  

**2. 自适应辅导与反馈系统**。Gao等（2025）设计了一种基于生成式AI的智能导师，并在高校物理课堂开展随机对照实验。结果显示，使用该AI导师的学生在学习效率上明显优于传统课堂学习，仅需更少时间即可达成更高的成绩，同时感知更高的参与感与动机水平 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-97652-6#:~:text=with%20an%20active%20learning%20class,broad%20adoption%20in%20learning%20environments))。该研究证明了结合研究性教学法和个性化AI辅导的潜力。其他研究则着重开发实时反馈和评估系统，如基于AI的编程提示或自动评分工具，以监控学习者状态并即时干预（相关工作参见Jogler et al.，2024）。  

**3. 游戏化与沉浸式环境**。Xu等（2025）提出一种带有游戏元素的翻转课堂（Gamified SRL Flipped Learning，GSRFL）模式：在学习管理系统中集成学习分析仪表盘和徽章、排行榜等激励机制，以促进学生在计划、执行、自我评估三个SRL阶段的行为。长期准实验结果表明，游戏化组的英语学习成绩和整体SRL行为显著提升，同时抑制了目标设定与时间管理等策略随时间衰退的趋势 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360131525000466#:~:text=regulated%20flipped%20learning%20approach%20,performance%20and%20promote%20SRL%20skills))。Wang等（2025）在虚拟现实（VR）环境中对比了基于ChatGPT反馈与传统反馈的学习效果。随机对照试验结果表明，结合GPT反馈的实验组在记忆、理解和应用层面的任务表现均显著优于对照组；GPT反馈显著提升了问题解决和批判性思维能力，且该组学生的SRL能力也得到明显增强 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/396242496_ChatGPT-enhanced_self-regulated_learning_in_programming_education_impacts_on_motivation_self-efficacy_and_learning_outcomes#:~:text=and%20foster%20higher,critical%20thinking%20skills%2C%20though%20its)) ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/396242496_ChatGPT-enhanced_self-regulated_learning_in_programming_education_impacts_on_motivation_self-efficacy_and_learning_outcomes#:~:text=demonstrated%20significantly%20higher%20cognitive%20levels,the%20experimental%20group%2C%20highlighting%20the))。另有研究评估以SRL策略为核心的沉浸式教育游戏。例如，Schmidt等（2025）开发的“Regulatia”游戏让预备师范生身处虚拟海底世界，必须运用SRL策略寻路归家。原型测试表明，该游戏具有良好的可用性和反馈机制，学习者主观感知到知识获得提升，视觉体验令人愉悦 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1875952124002040#:~:text=of%20the%20game%2C%20examining%20its,level%20progression%20of%20the%20game))，为进一步深度整合SRL支撑工具奠定基础。  

**4. 学习分析与SRL测评**。Liu等（2025）开发并验证了一种量表来量化学生在学习中使用AI工具的策略。通过两轮问卷（N=488和707）进行因子与Rasch分析，构建了包含“AI辅助任务处理调节”、“AI应用调节”等8个维度的自我调节AI使用度量工具。结果发现，学生最常用的是“面向输出的元认知调节”策略，而“AI支持的自我评估”策略使用率最低 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-025-00567-5#:~:text=analyses%20of%20the%20data%20identified,degree%20of%20AI%20exposure%20was))，暗示后者需要更有针对性的引导。这为精准诊断和引导AI驱动的学习策略提供了量化工具。总体来看，上述工作在不同层面探索了AI对SRL建模、监测和评估的可能性：从社交机器人长期跟踪儿童SRL，到结合眼动数据或脑电分析学习过程（相关综述见Li et al., 2023），学者们正尝试利用多模态数据和AI算法自动识别和促进SRL策略。

## 实验与评价总结  
上述研究普遍认为，适当设计的AI工具能够实质性改善学习效果和调节能力。多组实验证明，结合生成式AI的学习干预常常提高了知识掌握度、参与度和动机 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-97652-6#:~:text=with%20an%20active%20learning%20class,broad%20adoption%20in%20learning%20environments)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360131525000466#:~:text=regulated%20flipped%20learning%20approach%20,performance%20and%20promote%20SRL%20skills))；例如ChatGPT驱动的对话机器人相比传统机器人显著提升了学生的成就感和自我效能 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/379199414_Empowering_student_self-regulated_learning_and_science_education_through_ChatGPT_A_pioneering_pilot_study#:~:text=learning%20experience%20with%2074%20Secondary,reduced%20learning%20anxiety%20and%20promoted))。AI反馈与游戏化设计还促进了问题解决和批判性思维，并延缓了学生使用目标设定等策略的衰减 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/396242496_ChatGPT-enhanced_self-regulated_learning_in_programming_education_impacts_on_motivation_self-efficacy_and_learning_outcomes#:~:text=and%20foster%20higher,critical%20thinking%20skills%2C%20though%20its)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360131525000466#:~:text=regulated%20flipped%20learning%20approach%20,performance%20and%20promote%20SRL%20skills))。然而，这些研究也一致指出一些共性：学生普遍利用AI工具加强了认知与元认知调节（如获取提示、检索信息、监控学习进度），但对动机调节的支持相对有限 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00406-5#:~:text=conducted%20with%2016%20university%20students,aim%20of%20supporting%20students%E2%80%99%20SRL)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s41239-025-00548-8#:~:text=The%20main%20results%20revealed%20that,did%20not%20specify%20an%20SRL))；例如，刘等（2025）发现大多数学习者聚焦于“输出型”元认知策略而忽视“自我评估”策略 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-025-00567-5#:~:text=analyses%20of%20the%20data%20identified,degree%20of%20AI%20exposure%20was))。此外，也有研究警示过度依赖生成式AI可能导致学习者**被动**。意大利研究报告称，仅依赖AI完整答案的学生缺乏主动思考 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s40797-025-00354-1#:~:text=transformative%20potential%20in%20classrooms%20worldwide,that%20digital%20divides%20in%20AI))（虽在本综述时间窗之外发表，但与观察一致）。总的来看，实验数据表明：AI支持的SRL工具能够增强学习绩效和参与，尤其在认知和元认知层面[{27†L17-L24},{44†L39-L47}]；然而效果受到设计与使用方式影响，需注意维护学习者的主动性与反思过程 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41539-025-00319-0#:~:text=feedback%20systems%2C%20serious%20games%2C%20and,research%20to%20optimize%20this%20synergy)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s40797-025-00354-1#:~:text=transformative%20potential%20in%20classrooms%20worldwide,that%20digital%20divides%20in%20AI))。

## 趋势与挑战  
2025年前后，研究趋势将集中在以下几个方面：其一，更深度地将大规模语言模型与多模态环境（如VR、游戏、在线课堂）融合，以实现更自然的学习对话和更丰富的交互。例如已有工作将ChatGPT反馈嵌入VR学习，实现个性化辅导 ([www.researchgate.net](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/396242496_ChatGPT-enhanced_self-regulated_learning_in_programming_education_impacts_on_motivation_self-efficacy_and_learning_outcomes#:~:text=and%20foster%20higher,critical%20thinking%20skills%2C%20though%20its))。未来可能出现更多结合AI与沉浸式技术的创新SRL平台。其二，关注学习动机和自主性的培养。由于目前AI–SRL研究多聚焦认知支持 ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s41239-025-00548-8#:~:text=The%20main%20results%20revealed%20that,did%20not%20specify%20an%20SRL))，未来需开发能激发内在动机和自我效能的AI策略，比如利用即时反馈、奖励机制和社会性AI特殊交互来鼓励主动学习。其三，加强实时学习分析与个性化干预的开发。如引入过程挖掘和机器学习，从学习行为日志和生理数据中自动检测SRL状态，及时提供定制化建议 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/1999-5903/16/6/218#:~:text=This%20article%20seeks%20to%20adapt,applications%20of%20chatbots%20for%20SRL)) ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-025-00567-5#:~:text=analyses%20of%20the%20data%20identified,degree%20of%20AI%20exposure%20was))。其四，制定使用规范与教师培训。AI工具虽有助于SRL，但需避免削弱学生反思能力 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41539-025-00319-0#:~:text=feedback%20systems%2C%20serious%20games%2C%20and,research%20to%20optimize%20this%20synergy))；因此未来应形成教师AI素养提升方案和伦理指南，引导合理使用和融合，保障教学质量。最后，可预见更多大型实证研究和跨学科合作，将AI研究与教育心理理论紧密结合，共同应对AI赋能SRL过程中的公平性、隐私和伦理问题。

## 结论  
综合来看，2022–2025年AI在教育中支持自我调节学习的代表性研究多采用对话式机器人、自适应系统和游戏化界面等方法。总体结果表明，AI技术在提升学习成绩、元认知策略运用和学习参与度方面具有积极作用 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-97652-6#:~:text=with%20an%20active%20learning%20class,broad%20adoption%20in%20learning%20environments)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360131525000466#:~:text=regulated%20flipped%20learning%20approach%20,performance%20and%20promote%20SRL%20skills))，但其效果受具体设计与学习情境影响，学习动机支持不足是主要瓶颈 ([educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com](https://educationaltechnologyjournal.springeropen.com/articles/10.1186/s41239-023-00406-5#:~:text=conducted%20with%2016%20university%20students,aim%20of%20supporting%20students%E2%80%99%20SRL)) ([link.springer.com](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s41239-025-00548-8#:~:text=The%20main%20results%20revealed%20that,did%20not%20specify%20an%20SRL))。未来研究应更加关注人机协同的平衡设计，提升AI工具对动力和自我效能的促进作用，并开发健壮的监测工具和政策框架，以实现真正意义上的AI赋能型自我调节学习。
