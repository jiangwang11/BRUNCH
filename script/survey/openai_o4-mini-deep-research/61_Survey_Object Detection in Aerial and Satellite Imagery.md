# 引言 
航空与卫星影像中的目标检测具有独特挑战，如目标尺度极小、方向多样、背景复杂等。近年来深度学习取得进展，但“多尺度检测”、“旋转不变检测”、“弱标注/小目标检测”等问题依然突出 ([paperswithcode.com](https://paperswithcode.com/paper/remote-sensing-object-detection-meets-deep#:~:text=RSOD%20methods,also%20review%20the%20widely%20used)) ([pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37384469/#:~:text=With%20the%20rise%20of%20deep,Driving%20and%20Aerial%20scenarios%20respectively))。例如，Cheng等指出小目标固有视觉信息有限，传统检测器在此类目标上性能不佳，并提出了大规模小目标数据集SODA-A以促进研究 ([pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37384469/#:~:text=With%20the%20rise%20of%20deep,Driving%20and%20Aerial%20scenarios%20respectively))。Zhang等的综述将RSOD的关键挑战归纳为多尺度、旋转、弱监督和小目标检测等 ([paperswithcode.com](https://paperswithcode.com/paper/remote-sensing-object-detection-meets-deep#:~:text=RSOD%20methods,also%20review%20the%20widely%20used))。本文侧重2022–2025年间航空/卫星图像目标检测的代表性方法，围绕旋转感知检测、小目标检测、多模态融合检测等类别，对主要方法及实验结论进行综述，并展望未来趋势。

## 方法分类与代表作 

- **旋转敏感检测方法**：Yu等（2023）提出Spatial Transform Decoupling（STD），利用Vision Transformer对目标的长宽和角度分别预测，通过级联活动掩码强化RoI特征 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/spatial-transform-decoupling-for-oriented#:~:text=spatial%20invariance%20in%20the%20data,on%20the%20benchmark%20datasets%20including))。STD对位置、大小、角度分支解耦后逐步增强注意力，显著提高了模型对旋转不变性的利用。在DOTA-v1.0与HRSC2016基准上，STD分别实现了82.24%和98.55%的mAP，达到了当时的最佳成绩 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/spatial-transform-decoupling-for-oriented#:~:text=spatial%20invariance%20in%20the%20data,on%20the%20benchmark%20datasets%20including))。  
  Hang Xu等（2024）针对ROP检测中的角度边界突变问题提出双优化方案，他们将角度的“可逆性”与“连续性”分离处理，独立优化角度预测和其他参数的耦合平滑 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Xu_Rethinking_Boundary_Discontinuity_Problem_for_Oriented_Object_Detection_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=mode%20of%20the%20smoothing%20function,The))。该方法既纠正了角度边界不连续，又保持了角度参数与宽高参数的一致性，实验表明边界突变问题得到有效缓解，IoU平滑的效果明显提升 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Xu_Rethinking_Boundary_Discontinuity_Problem_for_Oriented_Object_Detection_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=mode%20of%20the%20smoothing%20function,The))。  
  Zeng等（2024）提出ARS-DETR，将Transformer应用于航空图像旋转检测。他设计了面向长宽比的圆周标签平滑（AR-CSL）和旋转可变形注意模块（RDA），根据目标长宽比给角度损失加权 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=40Br7dMyLz#:~:text=article%2C%20we%20propose%20an%20Aspect,precision%20oriented))。ARS-DETR旋转采样点对齐特征与预测，自动调整平滑策略，实现了对各类长宽比目标的高精度检测，在多项数据集上取得了竞争性性能 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=40Br7dMyLz#:~:text=article%2C%20we%20propose%20an%20Aspect,precision%20oriented))。  
  **单点弱监督旋转检测**：Luo等（2024）提出PointOBB，这是首个基于单点标注生成定向框的方法 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Luo_PointOBB_Learning_Oriented_Object_Detection_via_Single_Point_Supervision_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Single%20point,PointOBB)) ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Luo_PointOBB_Learning_Oriented_Object_Detection_via_Single_Point_Supervision_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Experimental%20results%20on%20the%20DIOR,supervised%20baselines))。PointOBB利用原图、缩放图和旋转/翻转图三种视图协同训练：缩放一致性损失提升尺度感知能力，旋转自监督模块预测目标角度 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Luo_PointOBB_Learning_Oriented_Object_Detection_via_Single_Point_Supervision_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Single%20point,PointOBB))。该方法在航空数据集DIOR-R和DOTA-v1.0上显著优于基于单点的水平框弱监督基线 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Luo_PointOBB_Learning_Oriented_Object_Detection_via_Single_Point_Supervision_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Experimental%20results%20on%20the%20DIOR,supervised%20baselines))。Yu等（2024）提出Point2RBox，将单点监督扩展到端到端定向检测。他通过对每个标注点周围采样构造带已知框的“合成视觉模式”提供回归知识，并引入几何变换一致性约束使预测跟随输入变换 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Yu_Point2RBox_Combine_Knowledge_from_Synthetic_Visual_Patterns_for_End-to-end_Oriented_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=point,To%20our%20best%20knowledge%20Point2RBox))。Point2RBox采用轻量网络实现，对照基线取得了可观性能（DOTA上41.05% mAP，DIOR上27.62%） ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Yu_Point2RBox_Combine_Knowledge_from_Synthetic_Visual_Patterns_for_End-to-end_Oriented_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=is%20the%20first%20end,on%20DOTA%2FDIOR%2FHRSC%20datasets))。

- **小目标及多尺度检测方法**：针对航空影像中普遍的微小目标，Yuan等（2023）提出CFINet，用于大规模小目标检测 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=alleviate%20the%20aforementioned%20issues%2C%20we,art%20performance%20on%20the)) ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=contrastive%20learning%20paradigm%20is%20devised,com%2Fshaunyuan22%2FCFINet))。CFINet在Faster R-CNN的基础上设计了Coarse-to-Fine RPN（CRPN），采用动态锚框选择和级联回归生成充足而高质量的小目标候选框 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=alleviate%20the%20aforementioned%20issues%2C%20we,art%20performance%20on%20the))。同时引入特征模仿分支，通过对比学习的辅助损失指导网络借鉴大目标特征来丰富小目标表征 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=alleviate%20the%20aforementioned%20issues%2C%20we,art%20performance%20on%20the))。在新构建的SODA-D（驾驶场景）和SODA-A（航空场景）数据集上，CFINet分别获得了显著的mAP提升，远超基础检测器和其他主流方法 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=contrastive%20learning%20paradigm%20is%20devised,com%2Fshaunyuan22%2FCFINet))。其他研究也采用多尺度融合或超分辨模块来增强小目标特征表示，但以CFINet为代表的多级候选框策略被证明对弥补小目标先验不足效果尤为突出 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=alleviate%20the%20aforementioned%20issues%2C%20we,art%20performance%20on%20the)) ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=contrastive%20learning%20paradigm%20is%20devised,com%2Fshaunyuan22%2FCFINet))。

- **多模态融合检测方法**：针对多源传感器数据，Chen等（2024）关注可见光与红外UAV影像的弱配准问题 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Chen_Weakly_Misalignment-free_Adaptive_Feature_Alignment_for_UAVs-based_Multimodal_Object_Detection_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Visible,modality%20spatial%20matching%20a))。他们提出偏移引导自适应特征对齐（OAFA）网络，通过跨模态空间偏移建模（CSOM）模块学习图像对之间的公共子空间偏移，从而对特征图进行可学习的位移校准 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Chen_Weakly_Misalignment-free_Adaptive_Feature_Alignment_for_UAVs-based_Multimodal_Object_Detection_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=Visible,modality%20spatial%20matching%20a))。OAFA自动调节多模态特征相对位置，有效缓解了模态间配准误差对检测精度的影响（无需严格匹配也可提升多模态检测性能）。  
  Zhang等（2024）提出E2E-MFD，构建一个端到端多模态融合检测器 ([papers.nips.cc](https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/5ddfb189c022a317ff1c72e6639079de-Abstract-Conference.html#:~:text=strategy%20in%20the%20gradient%20matrix,art%20approaches))。该方法在共享参数的基础上对各分支进行同步联合优化，避免了逐级训练的局部最优陷阱 ([papers.nips.cc](https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/5ddfb189c022a317ff1c72e6639079de-Abstract-Conference.html#:~:text=for%20multimodal%20fusion%20detection.%20E2E,impressive%20detection%20outcomes%2C%20such%20as))。实验显示，E2E-MFD对M3FD（水平检测）和DroneVehicle（旋转检测）数据集分别带来了3.9%和2.0%的mAP增益 ([papers.nips.cc](https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/5ddfb189c022a317ff1c72e6639079de-Abstract-Conference.html#:~:text=an%20optimal%20fusion%20detection%20configuration,art%20approaches))。  
  Li等（2024）提出SM3Det，首次定义了多模态数据与多任务检测（M2Det）挑战，并设计统一网络同时检测任意传感器下的水平或定向目标 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/sm3det-a-unified-model-for-multi-modal-remote#:~:text=With%20the%20rapid%20advancement%20of,task%20optimization.%20To%20address))。SM3Det集成了跨模态知识与多任务学习策略，以期充分利用不同数据源的共享信息，虽然细节展开尚在完善，其多模态跨任务的思路展示了融合外部先验的潜力 ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/sm3det-a-unified-model-for-multi-modal-remote#:~:text=With%20the%20rapid%20advancement%20of,task%20optimization.%20To%20address))。  
  Kumar等（2024）提出WildlifeMapper模型，特别针对航拍野生动物检测，旨在识别多种物种而非单一类别 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Kumar_WildlifeMapper_Aerial_Image_Analysis_for_Multi-Species_Detection_and_Identification_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=We%20introduce%20WildlifeMapper%20,accurately%20localize%20and%20identify%20them)) ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Kumar_WildlifeMapper_Aerial_Image_Analysis_for_Multi-Species_Detection_and_Identification_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=introduces%20two%20novel%20modules%20that,than%2011k%20Images%20and%2028k))。该方法引入局部结构与上下文感知模块来应对均质化背景，并在新的大规模野生动物数据集（11000+张图像，28000+实例）上检验性能，达到约0.56的mAP SOTA水平 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Kumar_WildlifeMapper_Aerial_Image_Analysis_for_Multi-Species_Detection_and_Identification_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=introduces%20two%20novel%20modules%20that,than%2011k%20Images%20and%2028k))。WildlifeMapper的成果表明，结合领域特定上下文信息和结构线索有助于复杂场景下的多类别检测 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Kumar_WildlifeMapper_Aerial_Image_Analysis_for_Multi-Species_Detection_and_Identification_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=We%20introduce%20WildlifeMapper%20,accurately%20localize%20and%20identify%20them)) ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Kumar_WildlifeMapper_Aerial_Image_Analysis_for_Multi-Species_Detection_and_Identification_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=introduces%20two%20novel%20modules%20that,than%2011k%20Images%20and%2028k))。

- **Transformer及端到端检测方法**：Zeng等（2023）将DETR结构用于航空定向目标检测，通过对角度标签引入长宽比敏感平滑和可旋转可变形注意力提升性能 ([openreview.net](https://openreview.net/forum?id=40Br7dMyLz#:~:text=article%2C%20we%20propose%20an%20Aspect,precision%20oriented))。Xu等（2024）提出的Dual-Optim角度优化思想也可以融入此类框架 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Xu_Rethinking_Boundary_Discontinuity_Problem_for_Oriented_Object_Detection_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=mode%20of%20the%20smoothing%20function,The))。此外，多模态融合网络如E2E-MFD通过端到端联合训练消除训练负担 ([papers.nips.cc](https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/5ddfb189c022a317ff1c72e6639079de-Abstract-Conference.html#:~:text=for%20multimodal%20fusion%20detection.%20E2E,impressive%20detection%20outcomes%2C%20such%20as))，展现了无缝融合感知与检测的能力。在基础模型方向，已出现自动化预训练策略（如多任务预训练、多模态对比学习等）以获取更泛化的特征。此外，已有尝试使用变压器混合架构（CNN+Transformer）或更轻量化注意力模块来兼顾性能与效率 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=Future%20Pharmacology%20Future%20Transportation%20Galaxies,Geosciences%20Geotechnics%20Geriatrics%20Germs%20Glacies)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=future%20model%20quantization%20and%20pruning,83))。

## 实验与评价总结 
上述方法普遍在DOTA、DIOR、NWPU VHR10、M3FD、DroneVehicle、SODA等数据集上进行评测，指标以mAP为主。趋势上，旋转检测方法（如STD、ARS-DETR）使航空影像的方向敏感检测精度显著提高，例如STD在DOTA上实现82.24% mAP ([www.catalyzex.com](https://www.catalyzex.com/paper/spatial-transform-decoupling-for-oriented#:~:text=spatial%20invariance%20in%20the%20data,on%20the%20benchmark%20datasets%20including))；单点弱监督方法虽然精度不及全标签方法，但Point2RBox等依旧在DOTA上达到40%+水平 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/html/Yu_Point2RBox_Combine_Knowledge_from_Synthetic_Visual_Patterns_for_End-to-end_Oriented_CVPR_2024_paper.html#:~:text=is%20the%20first%20end,on%20DOTA%2FDIOR%2FHRSC%20datasets))。小目标方面，专用策略使检测率明显提升：CFINet在SODA-D/A上取得领先结果 ([openaccess.thecvf.com](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/html/Yuan_Small_Object_Detection_via_Coarse-to-fine_Proposal_Generation_and_Imitation_Learning_ICCV_2023_paper.html#:~:text=contrastive%20learning%20paradigm%20is%20devised,com%2Fshaunyuan22%2FCFINet))。多模态融合方法通过对齐和联合优化带来跨模态信息增益，例如E2E-MFD在M3FD和DroneVehicle上分别提升典型mAP约4% ([papers.nips.cc](https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/5ddfb189c022a317ff1c72e6639079de-Abstract-Conference.html#:~:text=an%20optimal%20fusion%20detection%20configuration,art%20approaches))。总体而言，定向处理、特征对齐与多任务共享特征被反复验证为提升航空影像检测性能的有效手段。大多数研究报告相较于基线有数个百分点的增益，但仍需针对小目标效果、跨域泛化、安全性等方面做更全面评估 ([pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37384469/#:~:text=appearance%20and%20noisy%20representation%20caused,with%20a%20vast%20collection%20of)) ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-025-01078-8#:~:text=two%20stages%2C%20on%20meticulously%20curated,12%20EO%20tasks%20in%207))。

## 趋势与挑战 
面向2025年前后，有几个方向值得关注：一是**多模态与基础模型**的发展。大型多模态预训练模型（如SkySense++ ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-025-01078-8#:~:text=two%20stages%2C%20on%20meticulously%20curated,12%20EO%20tasks%20in%207))）显示，融合不同传感器和多阶段对比/掩码学习可大幅提升跨分类任务的表现。二是**弱监督与生成式技术**的应用。由于标注成本高，利用单点/少标注学习和生成数据增强将是热点 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=open%20research%20directions%2C%20including%20the,challenges%20that%20have%20limited%20SOD))。三是**跨域适应**和**可解释性**。现实应用中地物光谱变化大，模型需具备高度鲁棒性；联合考虑上下文关系和可解释推理（XAI）以减少误检也将成为趋势 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=,Additionally)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=match%20at%20L2125%20%2A%20Cross,detected%20objects%20closely%20to%20limit))。此外，**融合CNN与Transformer的混合架构**、**硬件感知模型压缩与实时推理优化**等也会获得更多关注 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/15/22/11882#:~:text=future%20model%20quantization%20and%20pruning,83)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/1424-8220/25/24/7563#:~:text=extraction%20of%20fine,resolution%20features%20from%20low))。

## 结论 
2022–2025年间，航空与卫星图像目标检测领域方法多样：针对图像高分辨率和旋转不变性，研究者提出了多种定向检测技术；针对极小目标，发展了多级候选框和特征增强方法；针对多传感器融合，设计了对齐与联合学习策略。实验结果显示，这些专门化改进能显著提高检测精度和鲁棒性，但在小目标难检、跨域迁移和算力受限等方面仍有挑战。未来应加强多模态基础模型、弱监督学习和高效网络设计等研究，以推动航空/卫星目标检测的进一步发展。
