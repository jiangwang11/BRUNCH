# 引言  
多无人机系统以其在覆盖范围、可靠性和效率方面的优势，广泛用于侦察、监视和精密喷洒等任务 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=Quadcopters%20are%20one%20of%20the,13%20Julian%20and%20Kochenderfer)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=protection%20UAV%20systems,Furthermore%2C%20by))。然而，由于无人机动力学高度非线性、多机耦合以及硬件和通信故障等问题，要同时保证编队控制的鲁棒性和性能成为关键挑战 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0096300324005344#:~:text=To%20tackle%20the%20trajectory,to%20their%20reference%20positions%20while)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=protection%20UAV%20systems,Furthermore%2C%20by))。此外，实际任务往往对轨迹跟踪精度、收敛时间、安全避障等性能提出严格约束 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=protection%20UAV%20systems,Furthermore%2C%20by)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0921889023002403#:~:text=The%20objective%20of%20this%20article,efficacy%20of%20the%20proposed%20approach))。针对这些问题，近期研究提出了多种容错与性能保障策略，包括分布式预测控制与滑模控制、基于神经网络或强化学习的自适应控制、事件触发控制以及基于屏障函数的安全控制等方法 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066123003040#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20problem,the%20total%20%2014%20and)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0921889023002403#:~:text=The%20objective%20of%20this%20article,efficacy%20of%20the%20proposed%20approach))。本文将按照方法类别综述2022–2025年间多无人机系统控制领域中具代表性的容错与性能约束工作，介绍其研究问题、核心方法和实验结论，并总结常见实验发现，最后展望未来趋势和挑战。

## 方法分类与代表作  
### 1. 分布式预测控制与滑模控制  
- Zhang等(2025)针对固定翼无人机编队的轨迹重规划提出了双环控制方案 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0096300324005344#:~:text=To%20tackle%20the%20trajectory,to%20their%20reference%20positions%20while)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0096300324005344#:~:text=collision%20evasion,the%20NMPC%20trajectory%20replanning%20algorithm))。外环采用非线性模型预测控制（NMPC），在考虑禁飞区和编队碰撞避免的同时，引导无人机逼近参考轨迹；内环则使用基于滑模的预测控制（SMPC）补偿执行器故障，跟踪外环输出的最优状态 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0096300324005344#:~:text=Firstly%2C%20in%20the%20outer%20loop%2C,a%20series%20of%20simulation%20results))。仿真结果表明该双环方法在容错性和轨迹误差方面明显优于单纯NMPC方案 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0096300324005344#:~:text=collision%20evasion,the%20NMPC%20trajectory%20replanning%20algorithm))。  
- Dong等(2025)针对有向通信拓扑下的动态领航编队跟踪提出了分布式层次化MPC结构 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0005109825000706#:~:text=This%20paper%20focuses%20on%20the,15%20method%20with%20an)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0005109825000706#:~:text=The%20intermediate%20layer%20enforces%20the,19%20regarding%20the%20selection%20of))。每架无人机配置三层控制器：外层结合Lyapunov-MPC与自适应估计生成平移控制量；中层控制姿态跟踪；内层输出快速收敛的角速度控制。这种设计在理论上证明了闭环稳定性并给出参数选取条件，仿真验证了动态领航任务下的有效性 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0005109825000706#:~:text=This%20paper%20focuses%20on%20the,15%20method%20with%20an)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0005109825000706#:~:text=The%20intermediate%20layer%20enforces%20the,19%20regarding%20the%20selection%20of))。  
- He等(2024)研究了在随机拓扑切换和外部扰动影响下的多智能体一致控制问题 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0947358024000669#:~:text=and%20ensuring%20satisfactory%20performance%20levels%2C,The%20proposed%20framework)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0947358024000669#:~:text=circumstances%20as%20well%20as%20switching,uncertainties%20within%20an%20unknown%20domain))。他们首先提出一种基于滑模控制与自适应算法的架构用于正常情况的轨迹跟踪；当执行器出现故障时，采用被动容错机制提高系统可靠性。仿真结果表明，新框架能在处理高度非线性动力学时实现一致性编队，减少颤振现象并保持对参考轨迹的准确跟踪 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0947358024000669#:~:text=and%20ensuring%20satisfactory%20performance%20levels%2C,The%20proposed%20framework)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0947358024000669#:~:text=circumstances%20as%20well%20as%20switching,uncertainties%20within%20an%20unknown%20domain))。  

### 2. 自适应与强化学习方法  
- Jiang等(2024)针对无人机群探索任务提出了一种容错多智能体强化学习算法 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=In%20the%20UAV%20swarm%2C%20the,process%2C%20the%20agents%20with%20poor)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=introduce%20a%20scenario%20regarding%20Multi,make%20the%20swarm%20perform%20better))。该算法允许无人机状态在执行过程中劣化，并将领域邻接矩阵和无人机健康度向量同时输入模型来更新通信拓扑。弱化无人机的健康状态被赋予更高关注，从而加速其恢复；此外引入时序卷积模块以捕捉动态信息 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=In%20the%20UAV%20swarm%2C%20the,process%2C%20the%20agents%20with%20poor)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=this%20limitation%20and%20allows%20the,ground%20exploration%2C%20where%20the%20health))。在一个无人机健康度逐渐下降并需定期救援的场景中，仿真结果表明该算法显著提高了无人机群的存活率和整体探索性能 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=introduce%20a%20scenario%20regarding%20Multi,make%20the%20swarm%20perform%20better))。  
- Zhang等(2025)开发了一种基于PSO优化的RBF神经网络滑模控制方法来提升单无人机的容错性能 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-13659-z#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20performance,Notably%2C%20this%20work)) ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-13659-z#:~:text=presents%20detailed%20simulation%20results%2C%20demonstrating,findings%20and%20proposes%20potential%20areas))。该方法利用RBF网络在线估计系统不确定性，从而减轻传统滑模控制颤振问题；并使用改进的粒子群优化算法调整RBF参数以加快收敛、提高学习稳定性 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-13659-z#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20performance,Notably%2C%20this%20work))。理论上基于Lyapunov证明了闭环信号有界，仿真显示与传统滑模相比，新方法在抗干扰性、容错性和鲁棒性方面都有显著优势 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-13659-z#:~:text=presents%20detailed%20simulation%20results%2C%20demonstrating,findings%20and%20proposes%20potential%20areas))。  
- Lan和陈(2023)研究了多无人机编队的最优跟踪控制问题，提出了一种结合滑模控制和Actor-Critic强化学习的框架 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066123003040#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20problem,the%20total%20%2014%20and)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066123003040#:~:text=cost%20function,Additionally%2C%20a%20formation%20tracking%20using))。首先利用滑模和神经网络设计自适应控制器以对抗模型不确定性和扰动；然后将编队跟踪问题转化为标称系统的最优控制问题，采用自适应神经网络和Actor-Critic结构递归近似最优策略。Lyapunov分析证明了闭环稳定性以及网络估计权重收敛性 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066123003040#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20problem,the%20total%20%2014%20and)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066123003040#:~:text=cost%20function,Additionally%2C%20a%20formation%20tracking%20using))。  

### 3. 事件触发控制策略  
- Mu等(2024)提出了一种基于事件触发的容错编队跟踪控制方法，保证预定义跟踪性能 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-446x/8/1/25#:~:text=This%20paper%20proposes%20an%20event,adaptive%20tracking%20controllers%20and%20event)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-446x/8/1/25#:~:text=triggering%20conditions%20to%20increase%20the,effectiveness%20of%20the%20theoretical%20analysis))。他们设计了具有预设误差收敛性能的自适应跟踪器，采用RBF神经网络补偿执行器故障和外部扰动影响，并结合事件触发机制提高控制计算效率。Lyapunov分析确保系统误差收敛且无Zeno现象，仿真验证了算法在故障条件下保持跟踪精度的有效性 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-446x/8/1/25#:~:text=This%20paper%20proposes%20an%20event,adaptive%20tracking%20controllers%20and%20event)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-446x/8/1/25#:~:text=triggering%20conditions%20to%20increase%20the,effectiveness%20of%20the%20theoretical%20analysis))。  
- Guo等(2023)研究了含通信故障情形的无人机群一致控制问题 ([www.oaepublish.com](https://www.oaepublish.com/articles/ir.2023.32#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20event,the%20effectiveness%20of%20our%20approach))。在部分无人机传感器失效条件下，他们设计了基于二阶一致性协议的事件触发控制律，并推导出系统收敛的充分条件 ([www.oaepublish.com](https://www.oaepublish.com/articles/ir.2023.32#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20event,the%20effectiveness%20of%20our%20approach))。仿真表明，该事件触发策略能有效减少采样频率，同时保证在通信故障情况下群体速度和位置的一致性收敛 ([www.oaepublish.com](https://www.oaepublish.com/articles/ir.2023.32#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20event,the%20effectiveness%20of%20our%20approach))。  
- Seo等(2024)针对存在输入约束和执行器故障的无人机编队，提出了一种自适应事件触发的有限时间容错围控方法 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0016003224007294#:~:text=This%20article%20investigates%20the%20distributed,the%20characteristic%20of%20adaptive%20adjustment)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0016003224007294#:~:text=The%20paper%20addressed%20adaptive%20event,containment%20control%20problem%2C%20in%20the))。他们利用平滑函数和努斯鲍姆函数处理非对称输入约束，将故障影响转化为可变增益控制问题；同时设计自适应触发阈值以应对通信限制。理论分析证明闭环系统的有限时间稳定性，仿真结果展示了在约束满足和故障扰动下系统轨迹快速收敛的能力 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0016003224007294#:~:text=This%20article%20investigates%20the%20distributed,the%20characteristic%20of%20adaptive%20adjustment)) ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0016003224007294#:~:text=The%20paper%20addressed%20adaptive%20event,containment%20control%20problem%2C%20in%20the))。  

### 4. 安全约束与性能保障  
- Huang等(2022)在农业植保场景下提出了性能保证的分布式编队控制方案 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=protection%20UAV%20systems,Furthermore%2C%20by)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=introducing%20with%20a%20collision%20prediction,constrained%20plant%20protection%20tasks))。他们引入了“预定时间性能函数”，使跟踪误差在有限时间内收敛至给定容限；通过二阶滤波器估计领航机信息并设计分布式协议，避开了有向图Asymmetric Laplacian矩阵的问题；并通过预测碰撞力场完成动态障碍规避。理论证明编队一致性可在满足预定性能指标的前提下实现，仿真验证了多无人机系统在预设时间内完成喷洒任务的能力 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=protection%20UAV%20systems,Furthermore%2C%20by)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9534514/#:~:text=introducing%20with%20a%20collision%20prediction,constrained%20plant%20protection%20tasks))。  
- Díaz-Rodríguez等(2024)基于控制屏障函数(CBF)和加速度场设计了一种轻量级多机安全控制算法 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0921889023002403#:~:text=The%20objective%20of%20this%20article,efficacy%20of%20the%20proposed%20approach))。该框架分两层：上层通过CBF和QP生成加速度参考，指导无人机避障并朝目标前进；下层使用线性控制律跟踪加速度指令。形式化分析给出了安全保证，相关仿真和实飞实验结果验证了该方法在保证避障一致性的前提下的实时性和有效性 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0921889023002403#:~:text=The%20objective%20of%20this%20article,efficacy%20of%20the%20proposed%20approach))。  
- Sadeghzadeh-Nokhodberiz等(2024)提出了一种基于屏障Lyapunov函数的分布式编队跟踪控制方案 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=Therefore%2C%20we%20propose%20a%20new,Owing%20to%20the)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=simulation%20results%20from%20the%20MATLAB,state%20error%2C%20and%20response%20time))。该方法在递推反步框架中对位置子系统设计BLF以保证碰撞避免，对姿态子系统设计另一屏障函数限制误差。分层控制结构使得最终闭环系统能在预设精度内完成编队跟踪。Matlab-Simulink仿真结果显示该方法具有高精度，并且相对于基于优化的方法计算开销更低、稳态误差和响应时间更优 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=Therefore%2C%20we%20propose%20a%20new,Owing%20to%20the)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=simulation%20results%20from%20the%20MATLAB,state%20error%2C%20and%20response%20time))。  

## 实验与评价总结  
以上代表性工作均通过仿真或实验验证了各自方法的有效性。总体来看，这些方案在含故障或扰动情况下均能保持编队稳定和准确跟踪，且性能约束设计有效控制了误差收敛行为。例如，Jiang等的强化学习方法显著提高了无人机群的存活率和任务完成质量 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/11/5/372#:~:text=introduce%20a%20scenario%20regarding%20Multi,make%20the%20swarm%20perform%20better))；Zhang等的MPSO-RBF-滑模控制在抗干扰和容错性方面优于传统滑模方案 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-13659-z#:~:text=presents%20detailed%20simulation%20results%2C%20demonstrating,findings%20and%20proposes%20potential%20areas))。事件触发机制在多篇研究中表现出对通信和计算开销的有效减少，同时保证了期望的收敛精度 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0016003224007294#:~:text=A%20novel%20adaptive%20event,maintaining%20the%20same%20triggering%20accuracy)) ([www.oaepublish.com](https://www.oaepublish.com/articles/ir.2023.32#:~:text=This%20paper%20investigates%20the%20event,the%20effectiveness%20of%20our%20approach))。基于障碍规避的安全控制则在保证群体安全运动方面展现出强健性和实时性 ([www.sciencedirect.com](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0921889023002403#:~:text=that%20computes%20an%20acceleration%20for,efficacy%20of%20the%20proposed%20approach)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11284313/#:~:text=quadcopter,state%20error%2C%20and%20response%20time))。这些结果共同表明，将自适应/学习策略与事件触发和性能约束相结合的容错控制框架，能在多故障和不确定性环境下保持无人机编队的健壮性和性能指标。

## 趋势与挑战  
- **学习与数据驱动强化**：未来将更多融合深度强化学习等数据驱动技术，实现在线故障诊断与自适应补偿。例如，将安全屏障函数与RL相结合（安全强化学习）已成为研究热点，用于兼顾灵活性与形式化安全保证。  
- **形式化性能保证**：利用控制屏障函数、屏障Lyapunov函数等方法对性能进行严格约束的研究将继续深化，包括显式的预设性能、定时收敛保证等。定量地融合控制设计与性能指标将是趋势。  
- **分层与多模态控制架构**：多层次、分布式容错控制架构受到关注，包括基于数字孪生的仿真辅助设计、系统级冗余与在线
