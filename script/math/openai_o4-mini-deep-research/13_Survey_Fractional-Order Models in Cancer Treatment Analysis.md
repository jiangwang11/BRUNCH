# 引言  
分数阶微积分通过其固有的记忆性和非局部特征，为肿瘤增长和治疗建模提供了新的视角。Carretero等人 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-37196-9#:~:text=fractional%20order%20Exponential%2C%20Logistic%2C%20Gompertz%2C,this%20case%20due%20to%20its))将指数、Logistic等经典肿瘤模型推广到分数阶形式，发现分数阶模型拟合肿瘤体积数据的均方误差显著低于整数阶模型（MSE减少至少一半），证明了分数阶建模的优势。近期研究普遍将分数阶模型与免疫疗法、靶向治疗及反馈控制相结合，以模拟和优化治疗方案 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=This%20research%20presents%20a%20novel,cells%20and%20mutated%20cancer%20cells)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=response%20to%20cancer%20cells,Furthermore%2C%20the))。本文综述了2022–2025年代表性工作，涵盖分数阶肿瘤-免疫相互作用模型、联合治疗与控制模型等多种方法，并总结常见仿真结论，最后展望未来研究趋势。

## 方法分类与代表作  

**1. 分数阶肿瘤–免疫相互作用模型：** 多篇工作采用分数阶模型描述肿瘤与免疫细胞的相互作用，并考察免疫调控方案。Mattei等 ([bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com](https://bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12874-023-02006-3#:~:text=In%20this%20study%2C%20we%20develop,world%20data)) ([bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com](https://bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12874-023-02006-3#:~:text=The%20findings%20of%20this%20study,and%20fractional%20optimal%20control%20problems))提出了针对肺癌的FTIIM-LC模型，在Caputo分数阶导数下刻画肿瘤和免疫细胞的动力学，并利用广义拉盖尔多项式(GLPs)方法求解。其核心方法是将模型方程展开为GLPs级数，通过拉格朗日乘子和分数阶矩阵运算计算近似解；数值仿真验证了不同分数阶下模型行为与理论结果一致 ([bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com](https://bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12874-023-02006-3#:~:text=In%20this%20study%2C%20we%20develop,world%20data)) ([bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com](https://bmcmedresmethodol.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12874-023-02006-3#:~:text=The%20findings%20of%20this%20study,and%20fractional%20optimal%20control%20problems))。Nisar等人 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11251283/#:~:text=In%20order%20to%20comprehend%20the,verified%20by%20the%20global%20derivative)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11251283/#:~:text=Reliability%20is%20achieved%20by%20employing,cancer%20cells.%20Investigating%20the))构建了一个包含IL-2、IL-12和抗PD-L1抑制剂的肺癌模型（TCDI\(IL_2 IL_{12}\)Z模型），研究免疫抑制状态下的肺癌传播与治疗。研究通过分形-分数阶算子和Mittag-Leffler核建立模型，证明系统的有界性与全局稳定性，并采用Lyapunov方法分析系统的全局稳定性 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11251283/#:~:text=In%20order%20to%20comprehend%20the,verified%20by%20the%20global%20derivative))。仿真结果表明，加入细胞因子和抗PD-L1后，免疫细胞活性增强，肿瘤细胞受到有效抑制 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11251283/#:~:text=Reliability%20is%20achieved%20by%20employing,cancer%20cells.%20Investigating%20the))。Uçar和Özdemir ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-3110/7/2/151#:~:text=In%20this%20study%2C%20we%20explore,has%20been%20observed%20that%20immune)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-3110/7/2/151#:~:text=system%20cells%20that%20are%20exposed,alpha%20%3D0.42))研究了IL-10和抗PD-L1联合给药对肿瘤-免疫系统的影响，构建含CD8\(^+\)T细胞、IL-10和抗PD-L1的分数阶模型，分别采用Caputo和Atangana–Baleanu (ABC) 导数分析系统。核心方法是基于ABM法求解和稳定性分析；结果显示在分数阶\(\alpha=0.42\)时，肿瘤细胞数量分别下降了约70.4%（Caputo）和80.2%（ABC），表明分数阶模型能够更精确地刻画免疫治疗下的肿瘤消退 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-3110/7/2/151#:~:text=using%20the%20Adams%E2%80%93Bashforth%E2%80%93Moulton%20%28ABM%29%20method,alpha%20%3D0.42))。Fismat等 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-024-82170-8#:~:text=Cancer%20encompasses%20various%20diseases%20characterized,The%20model%20examines%20parameters)) ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-024-82170-8#:~:text=cancer%E2%80%99s%20mathematical%20framework%20and%20behavior,comprehensive%20analysis%20validates%20the%20proposed))则提出一个肿瘤-免疫治疗模型，使用改进的He-拉普拉斯算法（HHLT）求解系统的分数阶微分方程。其分析框架结合了同伦变换和拉普拉斯变换技术，模拟了效应细胞与肿瘤细胞的动态演变，绘制了不同参数（如免疫细胞死亡率、肿瘤增长率、分数阶参数\(\beta\)）对系统行为的3D可视化结果 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-024-82170-8#:~:text=cancer%E2%80%99s%20complexities,making%20in%20cancer%20treatment))。Qayyum等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12474869/#:~:text=identify%20novel%20therapeutic%20targets,solution%20and%20analysis%20by%20creating)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12474869/#:~:text=multiple%20homotopies%20related%20to%20the,immune%20cells%20due%20to%20malignant))针对肿瘤-免疫和化疗药物浓度构建了模糊-分数阶模型，引入抑郁等不确定因素（使用三角模糊数处理初始条件）。该模型用Caputo分数阶导数描述含免疫效应细胞和化疗浓度的系统，并用改进的He–拉普拉斯–卡尔森算法求解。仿真结果表明：随着分数阶参数增大，效应器细胞和肿瘤细胞数量均显著增加，其中效应器细胞反应更为敏感，显示该方法在处理不确定性和捕捉非线性系统动态方面的可行性 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12474869/#:~:text=identify%20novel%20therapeutic%20targets,solution%20and%20analysis%20by%20creating)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12474869/#:~:text=multiple%20homotopies%20related%20to%20the,immune%20cells%20due%20to%20malignant))。

**2. 分数阶联合治疗与控制策略：** 若干研究关注联合疗法优化与反馈控制。Amilo等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=This%20paper%20addresses%20the%20dynamics,c%7D%29%20is%200.22%2C%20signifying)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=that%20cancer%20cells%20divide%20at,in%20its%20integration%20of%20surgery))设计了一个含手术和PD-L1免疫治疗的肺癌分数阶模型，通过引入Caputo导数和反馈控制系统评估两种疗法的协同作用。其方法是利用Pontryagin极大值原理确定最优剂量调节规则。结果计算出基本再生数\(R_0\approx2.6\)，治疗后生殖数\(R_c\approx0.22\)，表明肿瘤细胞分裂速率大幅下降；仿真显示联合治疗显著优于单一治疗，对癌干细胞增殖参数高度敏感 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=response%20to%20cancer%20cells,Furthermore%2C%20the)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=analysis%20highlights%20the%20sensitivity%20of,in%20its%20integration%20of%20surgery))。Amilo等（另一组） ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=This%20research%20presents%20a%20novel,cells%20and%20mutated%20cancer%20cells)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=tool%20for%20the%20development%20of,treatment%20approach%2C%20coupled%20with%20economic))构建了异质性肺癌的分数阶组合疗法模型，结合免疫疗法和靶向药物并使用PID反馈调控。该模型包含正常与突变细胞的相互作用及遗传突变影响，用于优化联合治疗以降低副作用。研究发现优化后的策略能同时实现原发肿瘤控制、转移抑制和免疫增强 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=This%20research%20presents%20a%20novel,cells%20and%20mutated%20cancer%20cells)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=tool%20for%20the%20development%20of,treatment%20approach%2C%20coupled%20with%20economic))。对乳腺癌而言，Abdelmohsen等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12277451/#:~:text=fractional%20scenario%20by%20incorporating%20the,points%20coincide%20with%20the%20fractional))提出了一个分数阶疾病模型，引入了化疗等治疗手段以及预防诊断变量，并进行了最优控制和敏感度分析。核心方法是基于Caputo导数构建五个状态变量的模型，并用实际数据拟合比较不同分数阶。结果显示，当分数阶约为0.98时模型与实测数据吻合最佳 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12277451/#:~:text=fractional%20scenario%20by%20incorporating%20the,points%20coincide%20with%20the%20fractional))。Khan等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11993709/#:~:text=This%20manuscript%20illustrates%20a%20fractional,of%20key%20parameters%2C%20including%20the)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11993709/#:~:text=fractional%20order,dynamics%2C%20potentially%20informing%20therapeutic%20strategies))则提出一个包含间歇性前列腺癌治疗的分数阶模型，在固定点理论下证明了解的存在性与唯一性，并通过分数阶Euler方法获得数值解。图形结果表明效应器细胞的杀伤速率和AD/AI细胞竞争参数显著影响肿瘤演化 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11993709/#:~:text=fractional%20order,dynamics%2C%20potentially%20informing%20therapeutic%20strategies))。Nisar等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12381137/#:~:text=In%20this%20paper%2C%20we%20developed,fractional%20operator%20with%20a%20power)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12381137/#:~:text=law%20kernel,the%20drug%20is%20the%20input))还设计了基于闭环反馈的癌症控制系统，将药物剂量作为输入、治疗细胞作为输出，证明引入Fractional Caputo模型能更准确预测不同分数阶值下治疗响应，并从控制论角度评估治疗方案的可控性和可观测性 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12381137/#:~:text=In%20this%20paper%2C%20we%20developed,fractional%20operator%20with%20a%20power)) **3. 分数阶模型的分析与数值方法：** 一些工作侧重数值解法和动力学分析。Gabrick等 ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.04446#:~:text=RTE,dynamics%20are%20separated%20by%20an)) ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.04446#:~:text=measures%2C%20which%20offer%20the%20advantage,which%20obeys%20the%20same%20relationship))对经典肿瘤模型进行了分数阶化处理，利用双曲映射评估混沌现象。结果表明，当分数阶α降低至0.9966左右时，该系统从混沌到周期运动发生转变，并发现分数阶与肿瘤增长参数呈指数关系，体现了记忆效应对混沌行为的抑制 ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.04446#:~:text=measures%2C%20which%20offer%20the%20advantage,which%20obeys%20the%20same%20relationship))。Vo和Ekpenyong ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.02589#:~:text=It%20has%20recently%20been%20discovered,viscoelastic%20models%20in%20more%20situations)) ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.02589#:~:text=steady%20state%20viscosity%20and%20elastic,cancer%20and%20immune%20system%20dysfunctions))研究了细胞粘弹性分数阶模型，发现分数阶Kelvin–Voigt模型能更鲁棒地拟合巨噬细胞对细胞骨架药物的响应，比整数阶模型捕捉到更多生物学信号。总之，这些研究提示分数阶方法在数据拟合与长期预测方面通常优于整数阶。

## 实验与评价总结  
各研究的仿真结果呈现出一些共性结论：首先，引入分数阶导数后，模型能够更好地重现复杂的生物动力学特征 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-37196-9#:~:text=fractional%20order%20Exponential%2C%20Logistic%2C%20Gompertz%2C,this%20case%20due%20to%20its))。例如，较低的分数阶参数往往导致系统响应更加缓慢，这正体现了更强的记忆效应 ([www.nature.com](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-16977-4#:~:text=The%20set%20of%20graphs%20in,be%20implemented%20using%20a%20fair))。其次，多篇工作均发现联合疗法或多模式治疗显著优于任何单一疗法 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=that%20cancer%20cells%20divide%20at,in%20its%20integration%20of%20surgery)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11395867/#:~:text=tool%20for%20the%20development%20of,treatment%20approach%2C%20coupled%20with%20economic))：如Amilo等 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=that%20cancer%20cells%20divide%20at,in%20its%20integration%20of%20surgery))报告手术+免疫组合疗法比单独手术或药物疗法效果更佳。第三，免疫治疗成分（如抗PD-L1抑制剂和细胞因子）普遍增强了免疫系统对肿瘤的抑制作用 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11251283/#:~:text=Reliability%20is%20achieved%20by%20employing,cancer%20cells.%20Investigating%20the)) ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-3110/7/2/151#:~:text=using%20the%20Adams%E2%80%93Bashforth%E2%80%93Moulton%20%28ABM%29%20method,alpha%20%3D0.42))；SimSun等证明添加IL-10和抗PD-L1显著加剧肿瘤细胞衰亡 ([www.mdpi.com](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-3110/7/2/151#:~:text=using%20the%20Adams%E2%80%93Bashforth%E2%80%93Moulton%20%28ABM%29%20method,alpha%20%3D0.42))。此外，灵敏度分析表明关键参数对模型行为影响显著：例如癌干细胞分裂率、免疫效应细胞杀伤率等参数的微小变化会改变治疗结果 ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10394091/#:~:text=that%20cancer%20cells%20divide%20at,in%20its%20integration%20of%20surgery)) ([pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11993709/#:~:text=fractional%20order,dynamics%2C%20potentially%20informing%20therapeutic%20strategies))。总体而言，这些研究一致强调：分数阶模型因其记忆性可更精细地模拟肿瘤-免疫与治疗过程，联合疗法通常带来更理想的肿瘤控制效果。

## 趋势与挑战  
展望2025年前后，分数阶肿瘤治疗分析领域可能呈现以下趋势：  
- **个性化参数估计：** 越来越多工作将结合临床和实验数据，以估计患者特异的分数阶导数阶数和模型参数，从而实现个性化仿真与治疗优化。机器学习等方法将可能被用于自动识别最佳分数阶模型与参数。  
- **跨尺度与空间建模：** 未来有望将分数阶方法推广到时空模型中（如分式扩散方程），考虑肿瘤微环境的空间异质性和血流动力学对药物分布的影响，以提高模型真实度。  
- **多物理耦合模型：** 分数阶框架有望与细胞机械性和代谢动力学等多种生物过程耦合，例如结合细胞粘弹性（见 ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.02589#:~:text=It%20has%20recently%20been%20discovered,viscoelastic%20models%20in%20more%20situations))），以揭示力学生理对治疗反应的作用。  
- **新型分数阶算子研究：** 尽管Caputo等常用，但对比研究（如Caputo-Fabrizio、ABC等无奇异核算子）在生物系统建模中的优劣也将成为研究热点，以选择最适合生物记忆特征的导数形式。  
- **实验与临床验证：** 随着建模方法成熟，应更多地与实验和临床研究交叉验证，评估模型预测的可行性和可靠性，推动分数阶模型在药物优化和疗法设计中的应用。

## 结论  
本综述介绍了近年分数阶微分方程在癌症治疗建模中的典型应用，涵盖了肿瘤-免疫动力学模型、联合治疗优化及数值分析方法等多个方向。综观已有研究，分数阶模型凭借其记忆特性能够更准确地模拟肿瘤进展与治疗响应，联合疗法模拟显示了较单一疗法更优的协同效果。未来研究将聚焦于个性化建模、时空耦合、多种分数阶算子的比较以及与临床数据的融合验证等，以期促进精准肿瘤治疗的数学方法创新。
